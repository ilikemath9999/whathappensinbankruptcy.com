What Is Filing Fee in Bankruptcy?

Home › Glossary › Filing Fee

The filing fee is the amount charged by the bankruptcy court to file a bankruptcy petition. As of 2024, the fees are $338 for Chapter 7, $313 for Chapter 13, and $1,738 for Chapter 11. Debtors who cannot afford the fee may request installment payments or, in Chapter 7, a fee waiver.

How It Works

Bankruptcy filing fees are set by the Judicial Conference of the United States and are the same in every federal district. The fee must be paid when the petition is filed, unless the debtor files an application to pay in installments or a fee waiver request.

Installment payments are available in all chapters. The debtor files an application (Official Form 103A) proposing to pay the fee in up to four installments within 120 days of filing (extendable to 180 days for cause). The court usually approves these applications routinely.

A complete fee waiver (Official Form 103B) is available only in Chapter 7 and only if the debtor's income is below 150% of the federal poverty guidelines. There is no fee waiver in Chapter 13, Chapter 11, or Chapter 12. If installments are approved but the debtor fails to pay, the case can be dismissed.

Key Facts

  • Chapter 7: $338 | Chapter 13: $313 | Chapter 11: $1,738 | Chapter 12: $278
  • Installment payments available in all chapters (up to 4 payments in 120 days)
  • Fee waivers only available in Chapter 7 for income below 150% of poverty guidelines
  • Fees are the same in every federal district
  • Failure to pay installments can result in case dismissal

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I get the filing fee waived?

Only in Chapter 7, and only if your household income is below 150% of the federal poverty guidelines. You must file Official Form 103B. There is no fee waiver available in Chapter 13 or Chapter 11.

Is the filing fee the only cost of filing bankruptcy?

No. The filing fee is the court cost only. Attorney fees, credit counseling courses, and financial management courses are additional costs. Total out-of-pocket costs for a Chapter 7 with an attorney typically range from $1,500-$3,500.

Do I have to pay the filing fee before my case starts?

Not necessarily. You can request installment payments and have the first payment due 30 days after filing. However, the petition is not fully filed until the fee is paid or an installment application is approved.
